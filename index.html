About the Entrepreneur

Ravi Kumar Sagar, 22, one of the youngest founders and CEO of RK'S INNO group was conferred with the most prestigious Dr. Abdul Kalam Seva Puraskar. The award was presented to him for his persistent service to society and is one of the youngest entrepreneurs in India. Dr. Kalam Seva Purasakar is hosted every year by Vandhe Bharat Foundation and LeadIndia Foundation to commemorate the birth anniversary of the late former President of India Dr. APJ Adul Kalam. The award is conferred to the deserving candidates on the birth anniversary of Dr. Kalam for recognizing various people who have been doing extraordinary work for society. RK Fabrics is the genesis of RK Group, which 42 years later has grown into a business conglomerate. It was founded by Ramesh Kumar P Shah in 1980, as a textile trading firm, based out of Bangalore, India and has become an established name today. The fundamentals of RK Fabrics is international textile trading and allied services like trend forecasting, design, stocking and garmenting; feeding into both the domestic as well as international markets. Our core strength lies in an extensively experienced team with solid credentials and fully equipped infrastructure. We are exclusive partners of Luthai China & Vietnam, Penfabric Malaysia, Stylem Japan and Monti India.
